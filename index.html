    10,000 unique Luna Apes landed on Terraverse and are ready to mark their district

    10,000 unique

    In the year of 2173, one of Earth's communications satellites received a signal containing detailed information about a mystical planet called Terraverse.

    It described a region of space time in which anything was possible, and invited all who dared to stake their claim there.

    Thousands of Earth's best citizens were chosen to explore this new realm.

    Known as the Luna Apes, these brave souls were dubbed as the best of Ape’s race and left everything behind to enter cryosleep and embark on a 100-year journey to the Terraverse.

    With 10,000 strong and cut off from their home planet, they are determined and ready to safeguard their new home from the dark forces that could endanger it.

    Whenever they aren't honing their fighting skills, they partner with others to build and support Terraverse communities.
